Meet James Blyzes

With over 20 years of professional experience in home remodeling & repair. I never shortcut any work I do and I offer a ONE YEAR WARRANTY on my labor! All of my paint jobs are backed by a limited 30 MONTH WARRANTY!

James Blyzes
Senior Handyman

When you’re looking for high-quality and personal service, you’ve come to the right place. At Aboveboard Handyman Services I will give you the attention and personal service you’ll come to expect and enjoy. I personally answer all calls and my pricing is straight forward and transparent. I also offer peace of mind by carrying $2 million in general liability insurance. A quality you will not usually find in most smaller handyman companies.

Let me share a little bit about me with you. Both my father and step-father were "Do-It-Yourself" men with the natural God given talent and ability to fix just about anything. I spent many days remodeling and repairing with them in my formative years. As a young adult I also worked for a general contractor & a roofing company picking up many more skills.

When I married, I moved into the corporate world and settled into IT (desktop/network support). During that time I continued the DIY legacy in my own homes. When a fortune 500 company laid me off in 2008 I decided to become master of my own destiny. Today, I'm the only one that can let me go.

I do my best to live my life treating others as I would like to be treated, with dignity, honesty & respect. I have reliable, dependable contacts in every aspect of home maintenance, remodeling and repair. If I can't confidently do a job, then I will put you in touch with a professional, vetted partner who will be able to complete the work. There is no charge for these referrals.

I currently service McKinney, Allen, Plano, North Dallas, Frisco, Little Elm, and surrounding areas. Email or call to see if I'm in your area!
